Solar Street and outdoor lighting is a dependable approach to offer illumination and security that is unconnected to the conventional electric grid where power outages often occurs. Solar Street lighting can reduce the hazards to public safety at night hours caused by cars and pedestrian traffic and curb vice appeal within an environment. It encourages a safe, more livable neighborhoods, brings your neighborhood together by creating well-lit areas that gives people confidence to stroll home at night, embark on early morning jog and visit play grounds. At Greensource Energy, our aim is to provide Solar lights where non-existed in communities, commercial centers, parks, Walk way, Street and Roadway. It's a long lasting sustainable solution and requires few maintenance visits, which is essential in difficult areas to reach.

  • Solar Street lights
