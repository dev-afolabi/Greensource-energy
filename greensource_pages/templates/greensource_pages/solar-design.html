01

Power is Generated

Modules absorbs radiant energy from daylight and is converted to Direct current (DC) which is then flows to the charge controller.

02

Energy is transferred

Direct current (DC) flows from the charge controller to the Battery Bank where the direct current is stored in form of electrical energy

03

Energy is converted

The Inverter draws the direc current (DC) and converts it to Alternating current which is what household appliances use.

04

Energy is ready to use

Your home is ready to use the energy generated supplied from the building's distribution board
