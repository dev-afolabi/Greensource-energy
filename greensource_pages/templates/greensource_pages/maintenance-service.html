All of the solar services we provide require maintenance (after sales support and services). We have a team of qualified solar energy technicians on hand to deal with any maintenance problems. Maintenance is needed for dependable and long-term energy output, as well as to ensure your system's optimal efficiency. Preventive and corrective maintenance are two forms of maintenance that we plan for our customers. Preventive maintenance is performed on systems that are still in use and are not expected to fail until being repaired. When a device reaches the point of failure before being repaired, it is subjected to corrective maintenance. All of our installations need maintenance to ensure that they last as long as possible and that they are running at peak performance. Cleaning solar panels, removing bad wires, cleaning batteries and replacing battery fluid for tubular batteries, air pressure cleaning of electrical components, and ensuring all cables are firmly fastened to their terminals are only a few of the maintenance tasks we conduct.
