We carry out Maintenance services for all Solar services we offer (after sales support and services). We have a team of experienced solar energy specialist ready to assist with all maintenance needs. Carrying out maintenance operations is necessary for a reliable and sustainable energy production and ensure optimum performance of your system. We schedule different maintenance plan for our customer which includes: Preventive maintenance and corrective maintenance. Preventive maintenance is done for systems still in operations and do not intend to eperience a breakdown before it is fixed. Corrective maintenance is done when a system runs to failure before it is corrcted. Maintenance is necesary for all our installations to ensure longeivity of the system, ensure the systems are efficient and functioning optimally. Our maintenance activities includes cleaning of solar panels, replacing bad cables, cleaning batteries and replacing battery fluid for tubular batteries, air pressure cleaning of electrical components, ensuring all cable are well fasted to thier terminal.
