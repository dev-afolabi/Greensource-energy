INTRODUCTION
Company History

Greensource Energy is an end-to-end, customer oriented, innovative green energy and management company providing cutting edge renewable energy solutions for residential, large and small businesses. Our aim is to design and install the best systems we can while offering value for money. Started in the year 2015 as an interest but established and Registered on the 14th of march 2019 as a professional renewable and alternative energy company.

What the company does

GreenSource Energy specializes in renewable energy involving solar energy system design and installation, electrical design and installation, HVAC design and installation, Energy audit/management, Project Management, and System maintenance.
