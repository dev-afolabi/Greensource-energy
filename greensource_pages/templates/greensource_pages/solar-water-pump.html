Solar submersible and surface pump is fast becoming a means for getting water from wells and boreholes as it reduces cost of burning fossil fuels and provides energy secuirty. We design and install for residential, commercial and more importantly agricultural activities. Our solar pumps are designed to produce electricity to lift water from wells using energy of the sun. We are committed to creating an eco-friendly system which can deliver clean energy at a reasonable price while reducing environmental impact of fossil fuels and cutting down on the cost of rising cost of fuels. Our design and installation is cost-effective in maintaining normal living conditions as it is guaranteed to give u water every single day irrespective of the weather cloudy, sunny or even when it rains. It can be used for residential purpose (domestic use), commercial purposes like community borehole, manufacturing production needing water, agricultural activities like irrigation, fishery activities, poultry and other farm work.
