We design and install solar panels for off-grid applications where there is no coventional light grid in the environment. This application is meant for rural areas and farms that are not connected to the national grid. Solar panels are designed to generate more than enough power which are stored in the battery for later use mostly at night. We ensure our off-grid system are well designed and sized to cater for cloudy and raining days to meet the energy demand.
We provide technical support and work smart to reach out directly with clients through one on one sales as well a number of other methods including phone consultations to provide suitable solutions based upon needs and desires of customer. Our aim is always for our customer's financial satisfaction, while ensuring we can achieve thier long-term energy goals and return on investment in areas not connected to the grid.
